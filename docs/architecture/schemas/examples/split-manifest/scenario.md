# Scenario: Escape Room Quest

<!-- anchor: game.meta.name -->
## Escape Room: Mystery Mansion

<!-- anchor: game.meta.description -->
You find yourself trapped in an old mansion. Solve puzzles and find the way out before time runs out.

---

## Introduction {#intro}

<!-- anchor: screen.intro.title -->
### Welcome to the Mystery

<!-- anchor: screen.intro.body -->
The door slams shut behind you. The only light comes from flickering candles scattered around the dusty room. On a table in the corner, you notice an old journal and a rusty key.

---

## Main Hall {#main-hall}

<!-- anchor: screen.main-hall.description -->
A grand hall with a crystal chandelier hanging from the ceiling. Two doors lead to other rooms: one to the Library, another to the Kitchen. A large portrait on the wall seems to be watching you.

<!-- anchor: item.journal.description -->
### The Journal
An old leather-bound journal. The last entry reads: "The secret lies where knowledge rests. Look for the numbers that make sense."

<!-- anchor: item.rusty-key.description -->
### Rusty Key
A small rusty key with an ornate handle. It looks like it might fit a cabinet or a drawer.

---

## Hints {#hints}

<!-- anchor: hint.library.books -->
**Hint: Library Books**
The books are arranged in a specific order. Notice the numbers on their spines.

<!-- anchor: hint.portrait.secret -->
**Hint: Portrait Secret**
The eyes of the portrait aren't just watching - they're pointing somewhere.

---

## Victory {#victory}

<!-- anchor: screen.victory.title -->
### Freedom!

<!-- anchor: screen.victory.body -->
Congratulations! You've solved all the puzzles and found the hidden exit. As you step into the sunlight, you can't help but wonder about the mansion's mysterious past.
